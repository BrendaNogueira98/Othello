# Othello
Work developed by Brenda Karoline Cruz Nogueira and Ana Filipa Pereira Torres during the subject of web technologies at the University of Porto in the class of 2020/2021.

Steps to use:
1.open on your IDE the the folder
2.open the terminal in your IDE
3.on the terminal write node index.js
4.open the folder outside the IDE and click on the file index.html

To play the game you must authenticate and choose an opponent that it can be your own computer or another player whoever logs in as well.
